About us

Founded in Lagos in the year 2018, our team consists of dynamic, energetic, charismatic programmers and designers who will turn your PSDs into pixel perfect images. We are slicemypsd and we will make your PSD conversions a breeze. We are enterprising designers who actively ensure the client's needs are put first, and continually provide quality service at affordable rates across Nigeria.
Our mission
To grow together with our customers by adding value to their businesses by providing high-quality work yet great value for money
Our vision
First and foremost PSD programmer of choice, while providing tailored needs to the clients and to consistently become the number one choice for markup in Nigeria

We get to work as if we go to adult playground, we do things effortlessly because we simply enjoy what we do!

Our office is like a sanctuary of positive vibe and energy. Here, our efforts flow seamlessly between everyone hence it is reflected in the projects that we work on. We strongly believe, a solid working team will result in fantastic results for our clients.

Having a bunch of young and talented people is not enough to measure the success of our organization. We factor in a trusting, open and friendly environment as crucial elements to make our work more productive. You deserve outstanding results all the time and this is what we will provide for you as the PSD conversion service provider of choice.
