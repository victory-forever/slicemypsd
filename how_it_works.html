How we work

The Evolution Process

Fret not, working with us has never been easier. When we receive your order, we can start working already based on the stages below

  • 1. Submission of work.

    The journey begins after submission of your corresponding design files like PSD, Sketch, Adobe XD, PNG, Ai, JPG, Indd, etc to us. Do remember to include all the requirements and instructions for the project.

  • 2. Letâ€™s Get Into The Details

    Now that we have all the details via your order, you will be assigned to one of our friendly experienced managers. He/She will personally assist you with a bit of human touch to understand every single detail we need to know to get a headstart for your project. We carefully look at your project specifications and most of the time, we would come up with recommendations on how to make your project better.

  • 3. Development - Let The Coding Begin!

    This is where the transition happens between our manager to one of our highly skilled developers. Our manager speaks in human language and the developer is the bridge to translate it into computer codings. Our project manager will selectively assign, the best fit for your PSD to HTML conversion and CMS and eCommerce implementation solution. We will also have our markup developers analyze the PSD files to come up with the best way to slice your design, while also planning for the HTML hierarchy and CSS structure. Concurrently, our CMS and eCommerce developers will outline a plan for further implementation. You can be rest assured that our developers will make your project a success. Our developers are trained to adhere to the high standard set by us at slicemypsd. We, at slicemypsd, continually work around the clock exploring new methods, technologies to serve our clients better.

  • 4. Time For Testing and QA - Almost there!

    Now, the time has come after the project is completed by our expert developers. Your project is then transferred to a quality assurance specialist who checks the integrity of the work making sure the code is bug-free. Our quality assurance specialist, working together with the development team, will ensure the results come out just as you described. They are our final bastion making sure the code is written according to our coding standards and with what you envisioned.

  • 5. Sneak Peek

    This is the moment you have been anticipating! Finally, the first preview of your project. At this stage, do let us know if there is anything you like to change.

  • 6. Tweak, Patch, And Fine-Tune

    There is a little bit of work left. Now, we will do a bit of tweaking and fine-tuning here and there. This is where we apply changes or fixes because we always strive to deliver the best outcome to our client.

  • 7. Done And Done!

    We are a happy service provider when our customer is happy with the final product! Once we get to the final version that fulfils what you had envisioned, we will attach a payment link. After payment completion, you will receive full access to all source files and our lifetime support.

  • Now You Are Ready To Make Your Visions Come True!

    Our number one priority is our client. We gladly receive with open arms any communication in the future, no matter how long has it been the project was completed. Be it a quick tweaking or a code bug, we are here to make sure your project runs smoothly. So, go ahead and do not hesitate to make contact with us for your PSD conversions!
