International bank transfer

Worldremit

You may make payment with Visa, MasterCard, American Express, JCB, Discover, and Diners Club using the PayPal payment module by following the procedure below:

1. View your invoice on your browser:
Login to your clientarea and click on the View Invoice button beside the invoice you would like to pay for.
